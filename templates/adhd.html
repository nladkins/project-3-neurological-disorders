The prevalence of neurological disorders has expanded over the years, including Attention Deficit and Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD). Not only can this have a debilitating effect on the individual, but this also affects the family members who are often providing the supports that are needed. The purpose of this website is to use datasets available from Our World in Data that is available on Neurological Disorder statistics across the globe.
